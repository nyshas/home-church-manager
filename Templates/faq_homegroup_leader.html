Frequently Asked Questions - Homegroup Leader



How do I add a person to a homegroup?

Click on the "Members" tab on the navigation bar, and then click "Add Member" at the top of the page.


How do I take attendance?

Click on "Attendance" tab on the navigation bar. Select the current date and time and click "Take Attendance". Click the check boxes next to a person's name if they are present. Leave blank if absent.


How do I edit/view past attendance reports?

On the "Attendance" page, click on "View Attendance History." Click "Edit" for the report you would like to view.


How do I edit my homegroup information?

Click on the "Homegroup Profile" tab, and edit your homegroup information using the form.
