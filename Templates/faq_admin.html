Frequently Asked Questions - Admin



How do I add a person to a homegroup?

Click on the "Homegroups" tab on the navigation bar. Select the "Members" button of the desired homegroup. Click "Add Member" at the top of the page.


How do I delete a homegroup?

Click on "Homegroups" tab. Select the "Delete" button of the desired homegroup. This will temporary deactivate the homegroup. To reactivate a homegroup, click on "Show Inactive Homegroups" at the top of the page. Select "Reactivate" on the desired homegroup.


How do I view all members of all homegroups?

Click on "Members" tab. Here, you can edit, create role, or remove a member.


How do I search by member demographics?

On the "All Members" page, click the "Advanced Search" button at the top. Click on the criteria you would like to filter by, and click "Query." This will display a list of members who meet the desired criteria. From here, you can email these members or download a report of the members.
